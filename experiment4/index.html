Experiment 4 - 3D Graphics

Description

For this experiment, my goal was to create an interactive simulation inspired by the three-body problem.

Controls
Mouse (right click): hold and drag to move camera
Mouse (left click): hold and drag to rotate camera
Mouse (scroll wheel): scroll up/down to zoom in/out

Reflection

I started out feeling overwhelmed by the difficulty of creating a physics simulation. It definitely was difficult, and there were times where I wanted to give up and try some other idea. I had to accept that my simulation was not going to be all that accurate. Ultimately, the visual and auditory experience was more important. One of the highs of this process was finally getting something reminiscent of the three-body problem working. It was so mesmerizing to see the spheres interact with each other. After hours of frustration, it made it seem all worth it. In the end, I'm happy with what I managed to put together.

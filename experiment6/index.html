Reflection

I had a particularly difficult time with this project because I didn't have a strong concept going into it. I wasn't exactly sure what data I wanted to visualize, or how my visualization would change how one experiences the data. So, I focused first on the mechanics of the visualization, then refined the concept with some outside help. One of the low points of this experience was realizing that my visualization didn't exactly suit the data I initially thought I would use. One of the high points, though, was realizing that visualizing wealth inequality with Plinko would not only make a lot of sense, but would be especially visually striking compared to a pie chart of the same data.
